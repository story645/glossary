# pullback bundle
- Given a bundle p:E→Xp \colon E \to X, and a morphism f:Y→Xf \colon Y \to X, then the pullback bundle f *E→Yf^\ast E \to Y is (if it exists) simply the pullback of pp along ff, regarded as a bundle over YY. [nlab]
